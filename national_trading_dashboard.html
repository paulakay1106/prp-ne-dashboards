Key Insights
📦 NA Sold vs Purchased
Purchased consistently runs ~2× Sold nationally. Feb 2026: Sold 2,432 vs Purchased 4,994. Both saw a step-change increase starting Jan 2025 — likely new members added nationally.
📊 PRP-NE vs National
PRP-NE volume far exceeds the national total — NE consistently represents ~70–75% of combined activity. National total grew from ~5–6k/mo in 2023–2024 to 7–9k/mo in 2025.
📈 Growth Trend
National totals jumped sharply from ~6,500 (Dec 2024) to 8,655 (Jan 2025) — a +32% surge. PRP-NE has remained relatively stable while national activity expanded.
